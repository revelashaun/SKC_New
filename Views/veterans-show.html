Southern Knights Cruisers Veterans Car Show

We are proud to present the 16th Annual Southern Knights Cruisers Veterans Car & Bike Show saluting and honoring our country's veterans. Money that is raised will benefit the Virginia Wounded Warrior Program, the Fisher House, and McGuire Veterans Hospital. We expect that there will be even more interest in the Show this year given the response we had last year...more to come on displays and vendors.


Because of your commitment, we continue to believe we can make a difference. To date $308,000   has been raised by this show.
